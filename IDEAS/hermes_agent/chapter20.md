# Chapter 20 — Sales Strategy — How Realtors Actually Buy

Selling this to realtors is less about the tech (you already have enough to win) and more about packaging, trust, and distribution. Most real estate tech fails not because it’s bad—but because agents don’t adopt “systems,” they adopt outcomes they understand in 10 seconds.
So the real question becomes:

How do you turn your AI system into something a realtor instantly believes will make them money? Let’s break it down into a practical go-to-market plan.
🧠 1. Reframe the Product (This is the most important step)
Right now you think you’re selling:
“AI SMS + voice automation system”
Realtors do NOT buy that.
They buy:

💰 “More deals from the same leads without hiring staff”
So your product becomes:
“A 24/7 AI inside sales assistant that responds to every lead in under 10 seconds and follows up until they book a showing.”
🏡 2. Define the Offer Clearly (Productization)
Don’t sell “bots.”
Sell a packaged outcome system:

👉 “AI Real Estate Assistant”
Includes:
🧲 Lead Conversion Engine
instant SMS reply to new leads
qualification questions
CRM auto-update
🔁 Follow-Up System
automated drip follow-ups
“cold lead revival”
📞 Missed Call Recovery
voicemail → transcription → instant SMS follow-up
📅 Showing Scheduler
auto-booking viewings
💡 3. Your REAL Advantage (Why you win)
You are NOT competing on “AI features.”
You are competing on:

⏱ Speed to lead
Most agents:
respond in 5–30 minutes (or never)
You:
respond in 5–10 seconds
That alone can increase conversion by 300–900% in real estate funnels (industry-known pattern).
🎯 4. Who You Sell To (Don’t go too broad)
Start with:
🎯 “High-lead-volume agents”
Target:
Zillow / Realtor.com lead buyers
solo agents doing 5–50 deals/year
small teams (2–10 agents)
Avoid initially:
luxury boutique firms (long sales cycles)
huge brokerages (procurement hell)
📦 5. MVP Product Packaging (What you actually sell)
You should NOT sell infrastructure.
You sell:

💼 “Done-for-you AI Assistant Setup”
Package includes:
Twilio number setup
CRM integration
SMS assistant trained on scripts
follow-up automation
voicemail system
onboarding + tuning
💸 6. Pricing Strategy (Simple and effective)
Avoid cheap SaaS pricing at first.
Go:

Option A — Setup + Monthly
Setup: $500–$2,000
Monthly: $100–$500 per agent
Option B — Performance angle (very powerful)
“We recover leads you’re currently losing”
optional bonus per booked showing
🧲 7. Your First Sales Strategy (IMPORTANT)
Do NOT start with ads.
Start with:

🥇 “manual pilot strategy”
Step 1:
Find 5–10 agents
Step 2:
Offer:
“I will install this for free for 14 days. If it doesn’t book you at least 1 extra showing, you don’t pay.”
Step 3:
Measure:
response time improvement
leads reactivated
showings booked
This becomes your case study engine.
📞 8. How You Actually Get Clients
Channel 1 — Cold SMS / Email (ironically your own system helps here)
Example pitch:
“I noticed you’re running Zillow leads. Most agents lose 30–60% of leads due to slow response time. I built a system that responds in under 10 seconds and follows up automatically. Want me to install it free for 14 days?” Channel 2 — Local brokerages
Walk in / call:
“I’m helping agents convert more of their online leads automatically.” Channel 3 — Social proof content
Post:
“How this agent booked 3 extra showings in 7 days using AI follow-up automation” 🧪 9. What You MUST Prove (or you won’t scale) Realtors don’t care about AI.
They care about:

KPIs you must track:
response time reduction
lead-to-showing conversion rate
reactivation of cold leads
missed call recovery rate
If you can show:
“We recovered 12 leads you were about to lose”
You win.
⚙️ 10. Productization (VERY IMPORTANT STEP)
Right now your system is flexible.
To sell it, you must make it:

“Opinionated and fixed”
Meaning:
same setup for every agent
limited customization
predictable onboarding
Avoid:
“we can do anything”
custom builds per client (scales badly)
🚀 11. Your Best Long-Term Positioning
You are NOT building:
a CRM
a chatbot
an AI assistant
You are building:
“A lead conversion layer that sits on top of existing real estate CRMs and increases revenue from existing traffic.” That positioning is MUCH easier to sell. 🧭 12. Suggested 30-Day Execution Plan Week 1 finalize MVP system get 1 internal demo working Week 2 onboard 1–2 test agents for free Week 3 collect metrics + testimonials Week 4 start charging first clients refine onboarding ⚠️ 13. Biggest Mistake to Avoid
Do NOT:
overbuild features
add multi-agent complexity
build SaaS dashboard too early
wait for “perfect system”
Realtor tech wins on:
speed to market + proof of revenue impact
