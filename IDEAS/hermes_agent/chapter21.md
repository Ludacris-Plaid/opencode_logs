🏡 RE-AI Go-To-Market System (Real Estate AI Assistant)
1. 🧠 Positioning (THIS IS EVERYTHING)
You are NOT selling AI.
You are NOT selling automation.

You are selling:

“A system that turns missed leads into booked showings automatically.”
One-line positioning:
“We help real estate agents respond to every lead in under 10 seconds and automatically follow up until they book a showing.” That’s it. No jargon. No “AI OS.” No “multi-agent system.”

2. 💼 The Offer (What You Actually Sell)
You sell a done-for-you conversion system, not software.
🧲 Offer Name:
“AI Lead Conversion System for Realtors”
📦 What’s included:
⚡ 1. Instant Lead Response System
SMS replies in <10 seconds
AI qualification questions
captures buyer intent automatically
🔁 2. Follow-Up Engine
automated follow-ups (2, 5, 10 days)
reactivates cold leads
stops when lead responds
📞 3. Missed Call Recovery
voicemail → transcription → instant SMS reply
never lose inbound calls again
📅 4. Showing Scheduler
books viewings automatically
syncs with Google Calendar
sends reminders
🧠 5. CRM Auto-Updater
logs every conversation
updates lead stage automatically
3. 💰 Pricing Model (Simple + Effective)
Start simple. No SaaS complexity yet.
🥇 Option A — Setup + Monthly
Setup: $500 – $2,000
Monthly: $150 – $500 per agent
🥈 Option B — “Performance Framing” (powerful)
“We recover leads you are currently losing”
Optional:
$ per booked showing bonus
or tiered success fee
⚠️ Rule:
Do NOT underprice.
Realtors already spend:

$500–$5,000/month on leads
You’re improving ROI, not replacing ads.

5. 📞 Demo Script (THIS CLOSES DEALS)
You do NOT demo features.
You demo MONEY.

Step 1 — Pain Hook
“How fast do you respond to new leads right now?”
(They say: “minutes / hours / depends”)
Step 2 — Shock stat
“Most agents lose 40–60% of leads just from slow response time.” Step 3 — Show system “This system replies in under 10 seconds automatically.” Show SMS coming in → instant reply. Step 4 — Follow-up magic “And if they don’t respond, it keeps following up every few days.” Step 5 — Close “This is already recovering leads agents thought were dead.”
